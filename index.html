Martin Lass - Violinist

Forget 2Cellos, The Piano Guys, David Garrett, Andre Rieu, and Lindsay Stirling... well before them, Martin Lass had already broken the mold of the usual violin soloist, crossing over from a classical background into his ancestral gypsy music, rocked-up Bach, and breathtaking original compositions.

He won the Australian “Mo Award” for Instrumental Excellence seven times in a row, was awarded the “Australian Variety Club Showman of the Year,” has performed alongside the likes of Julio Iglesias and Luciano Pavarotti, and has performed in three British Royal Command Galas.
